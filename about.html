About Me

Who am I?

I am an aspiring front-end developer based in South Texas. I started learning web development back in 2018 as a hobby, but decide to turn a hobby into a possible career. I enjoy the creativity and challenges that come with learning web development and am always learning every day.

What is this website?

First and foremost I grew up in the 90s and still listen to golden age hip-hop. Secondly, anyone who lived during the 90s can remember the geocities websites that many a young teen created. Unfortunately, due to a lack of a financial portfolio, I was unable to leave my mark on the young digital landscape of the Internet (at the time), but now that my financial portfolio is growing it really isn't, I can finally recreate all the 90s websites to my hearts content. Honestly, I just really like the vaporwave style and wanted to make a website in a vaporwave format.

Skills & Misc.

I have familiarity with JS, jQuery, HTML, CSS3, SASS & Bootstrap4. I also have experience utilizing Git and Photoshop when needed.
